Characters

Here are some of the main characters:

  • Hiccup - The main character
  • Toothless - Hiccup's dragon
  • Astrid - Hiccup's girlfriend and a talented viking
  • Stoick - Chief viking and Hiccup's father
  • Gobber - Blacksmith and Stoicks best friend
  • Snotlout, Fishleg, Inerman, Tuffnut, Thorston - Hiccup's teenage friends in the village




Summary

The movie is set in Viking times, where they didn't have modern tools or guns. Vikings lived in a place called Berk and often faced attacks from dragons who wanted their animals. Stoick is the village chief, while Hiccup, his son, doesn't quite fit in as a typical Viking.

During raids, there's a mysterious dragon, Night Fury, that's super fast and hard to catch. Vikings struggle to deal with it.

In a raid, Hiccup manages to bring down a Night Fury, but he can't bring himself to harm it. Instead, he names it Toothless and befriends it. Hiccup learns that dragons aren't just wild creatures and even invents a way to fly on Toothless.

Hiccup joins dragon training with other young Vikings, including his crush, Astrid. Despite ridicule from his peers, he uses what he's learned from Toothless to excel in training.

Tensions rise between Vikings and dragons, and Hiccup discovers a giant dragon, the Red Death, is behind the raids. It controls other dragons to steal food.

Hiccup, along with his friends and Toothless, plans to defeat the Red Death. They free the dragons and lead them to safety, ultimately facing off against the Red Death and winning.

This victory changes everything. Vikings and dragons become allies, thanks to Hiccup's bravery. They start living together in the same village.

Hiccup's journey teaches us that understanding and empathy can bring even the most different beings together. He not only forms a bond between Vikings and dragons but also defeats the Red Death, the dragon influencing the others to attack and steal from the village.
